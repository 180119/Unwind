ABOUT US

Hi, Welcome to the UNWIND About page. We at UNWIND consist of a team of four students. Carlijn, Emma, Floor and Tess. We are all 2nd year IMEM students at BUas. Together we set up UNWIND and we have grown into a successful business that, in collaboration with BUas, help students who experience stress. UNWIND was created after a major investigation into student problems. We also found out from our own experiences that an HBO study, in particular IMEM, can be very stressful. Everyone has their own way of dealing with this stress, but we saw that no company offered a solution. And that's how UNWIND was born, meant to help students deal with stress in a simple and cheap way.
