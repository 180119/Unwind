UNWIND THE BRAND


our vision

“In a society of perfectionism we tend to drown in our endless attempts of achieving an unrealistic ideal. We believe in getting rid of impossible standards, only then we can feel free of pressure and exist freely.”


“UNWIND” is a new brand that stands for balance, confidence, liberty and a positive outlook. As students we feel pressured to achieve the highest possible result and to know exactly what we want in life. As a result, many students encounter stress about their academic results and their future. Quitting is not an option because that would be seen as failure. We are not alone in this and therefore we want to raise awareness about this problem.

INFO ABOUT STRESS

Stress is the body's reaction to any change that requires an adjustment or response. It is a natural feeling of not being able to cope with specific demands and events. However, stress can become a chronic condition if a person does not take steps to manage it. Then it can undermine a person's mental and physical health and become harmful. Therefore, UNWIND wants to help you deal with this stress more easily. We specifically focus on stress caused by school. This can be caused by an excessive workload, an inconvenient schedule or other external factors. Are you a student who feels stressed? Then UNWIND is something for you!
